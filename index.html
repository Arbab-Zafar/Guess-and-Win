HOW TO PLAY GAME :-

The game will generate a RANDOM NUMBER.You have to GUESS the number in the given CHANCES given according to the AMOUNT paid.If you GUESS the NUMBER in the given CHANCES then you will WIN and the WINNING AMOUNT will be GIVEN to YOU.If "YOU ARE CLOSE" displays then the the RANDOM NUMBER is within the RANGE of 3

The Price, CHANCES and WINNING amount of the game are as follows:-

1->Give 1 RUPEES and get 4, 5 or 6 CHANCES. If won - 2 RUPEES!
2->Give 2 RUPEES and get 4, 5 or 6 CHANCES. If won - 4 RUPEES!
3->Give 5 RUPEES and get 4 or 5 CHANCES. If won - 10 RUPEES!
